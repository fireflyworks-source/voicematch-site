Voice Match — Privacy Policy

Effective July 7, 2026 · Firefly Works

Voice recordings

Your recordings are sent to our server over an encrypted connection, analyzed in memory, and immediately discarded. We do not store your voice, we do not use it to train models, and no human hears it. A short recording may be processed by an AI model to generate the written description of your voice; it is not retained by that provider either.

What we store

We do not require accounts and do not store personal profiles. Your results history is saved only on your device and is deleted when you delete the app. If you submit a character suggestion, we store only the text you typed — nothing that identifies you.

Purchases

Payments are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. We may verify purchase receipts with Apple to unlock content you bought.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. Our server keeps standard short-lived technical logs (such as request timestamps and status codes) for reliability and abuse prevention.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.
